Lauren Huang

Holidays

Holidays. When I think of them, my mind swirls back to those hazy nights when I would carefully descend the stairs to the living room and stare up at the little fabric Santa poking out of the fabric chimney that was nestled atop the lit Christmas tree. I fondly recall the days when my mom would take time off of work to sit and laugh with my brother and me while we frosted together pieces of gingerbread for gingerbread houses.
